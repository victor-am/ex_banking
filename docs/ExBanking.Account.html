    ex_banking v0.1.0 ExBanking.Account

    The Account module is responsible for handling interactions between the ExBanking functions and the GenServer implemented in AccountServer.

    It handles some business logic while also translating the low-level messages from AccountServer to business messages that ExBanking can actually return.
